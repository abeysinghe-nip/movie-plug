FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How to create a Login account

To create a login account, click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in the required information. Once you submit the form, your account will be created, and you can use the credentials to log in.

How to get Premium Access

To get Premium Access, upgrade your account to a premium subscription plan. Go to your account settings and select the premium plan. With Premium Access, you can enjoy ad-free streaming and exclusive content.

Can I download movies for offline viewing?

Yes, you can download movies for offline viewing with our premium subscription. Simply click on the download button next to the movie, and it will be available to watch offline on your device.



Are there any discounts or offers?

We frequently offer discounts and special offers to our users. Keep an eye on our promotions page and subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated with the latest deals and offers.

How often are new movies added?

We add new movies to our collection regularly. You can expect fresh content every week, including the latest releases and popular classics. Stay tuned for our monthly movie recommendations and updates.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Once the subscription is cancelled, you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you instructions to reset your password.



Can I watch movies on multiple devices?

Yes, you can watch movies on multiple devices with the same account. Your account is synced across all devices, allowing you to resume watching from where you left off.
